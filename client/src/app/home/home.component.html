FAQ

What?

WrongOpinions is a system that analyses your MyAnimeList profile and uses statistics to automatically detect objectively incorrect anime-related opinions that you hold.

You can kind of think of it as like The Anime Man's "Roasting _____'s HORRIBLE Taste in Anime" series, but for people who Joey doesn't even know exist. (Like you)

WrongOpinions generates a sharable infographic explaining your worst takes, so you can show off your awful taste on social media like some misguided badge of pride.

Why?

If I'm talking to someone and I find out they watch anime, naturally I ask "What kind of anime do you like?". But this usually has a boring answer. I don't care that you like FMA:B, Violet Evergarden, or Steins;Gate, everyone does.

Someone's taste in anime is better understood by their controversial takes. What's a popular anime that you hate, or an unpopular anime that you love? Maybe I'll learn something about you, or maybe it'll just start a fun argument.

Is that the reason, or just an excuse? Truthfully, a lot of you just have terrible taste and I don't have time to point that out to you individually. Automating the process was necessary.

WrongOpinions was wrong

WrongOpinions isn't perfect. For example, WrongOpinions even seems to think that some of my opinions are wrong, but that's obviously not true. All of my opinions are objectively correct.

While I have tried hard to crunch data in ways that are statistically-sound, I had to balance that against entertainment value. WrongOpinions is designed to generate spicy insults, and was balanced such that it should be able to produce something of interest for most users. Additionally, I don't actually have any background in statistics, so I mostly just Googled how to use a lot of these formulas.

That's impossible, stop lying. WrongOpinions uses statistics to identify objectively incorrect opinions. As the arbiter of truth, it is incapable of being wrong itself. There will be no second opinions.
